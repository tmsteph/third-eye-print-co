How the quote flow works

Simple on purpose. The client should understand the process in under twenty seconds.

01

Send the job

Share the product, quantity, timing, and artwork status. If the job spans multiple categories, that is fine.

02

Review the scope

Third Eye confirms the production path, asks for any missing specs, and sends back pricing or proof guidance.

03

Approve and produce

Once scope and proof are approved, the order moves into production, pickup, shipping, or sourced fulfillment.

Quick answers

What files should clients send?

Vector files are best: AI, EPS, SVG, or PDF. High-resolution PNG files still work for many jobs.

Can mixed orders be quoted?

Yes. Apparel, handouts, stickers, and signage can be quoted together when the client wants one coordinated project.

Is everything produced in-house?

T-shirts are produced in-house. Depending on the project, some items may be sourced through trusted partners.
