Quick answers

Enough detail to reduce friction without overexplaining the operation.

What files should clients send?

Vector files are best: AI, EPS, SVG, or PDF. High-resolution PNG files still work for many jobs.

Can mixed orders be quoted?

Yes. Apparel, handouts, stickers, and signage can be quoted together when the client wants one coordinated project.

Is everything produced in-house?

No. The site is positioned to allow trusted trade fulfillment where that creates a cleaner result for the client.
